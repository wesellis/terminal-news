## The Honest Reality Check

### What makes this viable:

✅ **Proven business model** (Craigslist makes $1B/year on classifieds)
✅ **Low overhead** (no inventory, no employees initially)
✅ **Recurring revenue** (sponsors pay monthly)
✅ **Scalable** (software scales better than services)
✅ **Niche defensibility** (terminal users are loyal)
✅ **Multiple revenue streams** (not dependent on one source)

### What makes this risky:

⚠️ **Network effects required** (need critical mass in each city)
⚠️ **Moderation burden** (spam, scams, abuse)
⚠️ **Competitive response** (what if Craigslist adds this?)
⚠️ **Niche market** (terminal users are small % of population)
⚠️ **Execution risk** (you have to build it well)
⚠️ **Time to revenue** (6+ months minimum)
